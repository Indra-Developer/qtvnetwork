Data Sovereignty Protocol

Privacy
Manifesto.

At QTV Network, we believe privacy is a fundamental right, not a feature. This manifesto outlines our technical and ethical commitment to protecting your digital footprint.

Section 01 // Doctrine

The Sovereign Citizen

The QTV Network, architected by Zigzex IT Services, operates under a "Zero-Retention" philosophy where possible. We recognize that our audience in Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, and globally deserves a broadcast experience that does not commodify their personal data for third-party gain.

Transparency First

Every byte of data we collect is explicitly declared. We do not engage in "shadow-tracking" or cross-domain user profiling.

Ethical Integrity

Our monetization models are built on premium content and authorized advertising, never on the sale of student or devotee data.

Section 02 // Architecture

Information Vectors

We classify data collection into three specific vectors to maintain operational efficiency while ensuring user anonymity.

  • Broadcast Analytics

    We collect anonymous telemetry (IP region, device type, stream quality) to optimize our global CDN routing. This data is never linked to a specific individual.

  • Active Engagement

    When you utilize our "Secure Dispatch" or "Whistleblower Tip Line," we encrypt your input at the browser level before it reaches our editorial team.

  • Operational Cookies

    We use essential cookies to remember your theme preferences (Light/Dark) and your region to serve the correct local news feeds.

Section 03 // Infrastructure

Encryption Hierarchy

To ensure the safety of students reporting campus issues and sources providing sensitive news tips, we employ a multi-layered security stack.

TLS 1.3 End-to-End

Every interaction between your browser and the QTV Network is secured via high-grade TLS encryption, preventing man-in-the-middle interceptions on public or university networks.

Section 04 // Partners

External Ecosystems

QTV utilizes world-class infrastructure partners (AWS, Google Cloud, WhatsApp) to deliver high-fidelity content. Each partner is strictly vetted for compliance with global data protection standards.

When you click a YouTube stream or a WhatsApp dispatch link, you are entering those respective ecosystems. We encourage you to review their specific privacy policies as they pertain to their individual platforms.

Section 05 // Sovereignty

User Agency

Regardless of your location, we honor the following digital rights for all users of the QTV Network:

Right to Access

Query your data

Right to Erasure

Permanent deletion

Right to Object

Opt-out anytime

Section 06 // Retention

Data Lifespan

We do not believe in hoarding data. Operational logs are purged every 90 days. Anonymous analytics are aggregated and stripped of PII (Personally Identifiable Information) immediately. Secure tips are retained only for the duration of the journalistic investigation before being moved to offline, encrypted cold storage.

Section 07 // Governance

Inquiries & Compliance

For questions regarding our Privacy Manifesto or to exercise your digital rights, contact our Data Protection Officer (DPO) at our Srikakulam Nerve Center.
